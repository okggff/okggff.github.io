About Our Club

The Night Owl Basketball Club is for kids and teens who love playing basketball at night!! We have exciting games every week and everyone can join from beginners to pros!!!

  • USA Basketball rules

we follow these rules so our games are fair, safe, and fun for everyone.

Benefits of Joining

  • Play basketball with friends
  • learn new skills and improve
  • Meet new people and have fun
  • Participate in tournaments
  • Get coaching tips from experienced players

Everyone can join and enjoy the game no matter your skill level!
